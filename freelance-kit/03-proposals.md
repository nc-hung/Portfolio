# Proposal Templates — paste-and-tweak (EN)

> Trên Upwork, **proposal quyết định 80%**. Khách đọc 2 dòng đầu rồi quyết đọc tiếp hay bỏ.
> 5 quy tắc bất biến:
> 1. **2 câu đầu nói VỀ HỌ**, không về bạn. Nhắc lại đúng pain trong JD.
> 2. **1 case study gần nhất**, kèm 1 con số nếu có.
> 3. **1 câu hỏi thông minh** → ép khách reply.
> 4. **Ngắn** (120–180 từ). Khách không đọc essay.
> 5. **Không** "Dear Sir/Madam", không "I am passionate", không liệt kê cả CV.

---

## T1 — Invoicing / Billing / Ledger / Reconciliation jobs (ngách A)

```
Hi [Name],

Getting payment reconciliation right is exactly the work I specialize in —
the part where a rounding error or a wrongly-applied payment quietly corrupts
a balance. You mentioned [their specific pain, e.g. "matching payments to
invoices manually"]; that's solvable and safe to automate.

I recently built an order & debt platform for a wholesale business: payments
auto-allocate across invoices with FIFO logic, every amount uses exact decimal
math (never floats), and statements export print/QR-ready in one click. It
replaced hours of manual spreadsheet reconciliation.

For yours: are payments applied oldest-invoice-first (FIFO), or do customers
specify which invoice each payment covers? That changes the allocation model.

Happy to share a quick walkthrough of the reconciliation flow if useful.

— Hung
```

## T2 — AI automation / chatbot / integration / Excel jobs (ngách B)

```
Hi [Name],

This is squarely what I do — wiring a [POS/CRM/messaging app] into an automated
flow so the manual work disappears. You described [their pain, e.g. "manually
sending payment reminders"]; that's a clean automation win.

I built a sales bot integrated with a POS that pushes live dashboards, generates
charts with AI, issues QR-code payments, and sends scheduled reminders
automatically — deployed and running on Railway.

One question to scope it right: do you want this fully autonomous, or with a
human-approval step before anything is sent to a customer?

I can map out the integration in a short call and give you a fixed quote.

— Hung
```

## T3 — Admin dashboard / internal tool / general full-stack jobs

```
Hi [Name],

You need [internal tool / dashboard] that your team will actually use daily —
fast, clear, and built on data you can trust. That last part is where most
builds fall short, and it's my strength.

I build internal systems end to end: React + MUI front end, a clean Node/Prisma
API, real deployment with Docker/CI. Recent work includes an admin platform for
order, payment, and debt tracking with role-based access and audit logs.

Quick scoping question: roughly how many users, and do you need roles/permissions
(e.g. admin vs staff views)?

Send me the must-have screens and I'll come back with a plan and timeline.

— Hung
```

## T4 — Fix / rescue an existing system (cực kỳ dễ chốt, ít cạnh tranh)

```
Hi [Name],

Inheriting a system that's [buggy / slow / half-finished] is frustrating — and
fixable. I'm good at reading unfamiliar codebases fast and stabilizing them
without a rewrite.

My background is full-stack TypeScript (Node/Express/NestJS + React) with a lot
of time spent on the money-correct, data-integrity parts that tend to break.

To give you a real answer: is the repo accessible, and what's the single most
painful symptom right now? I'll start there and tell you honestly whether it's
a quick fix or needs more.

— Hung
```

### Sau khi gửi
- Theo dõi: gửi / xem / reply. Sau 20 proposal mà reply <1 → đổi 2 câu đầu (vấn đề nằm ở hook).
- Khách reply → trả lời trong **<1 giờ** nếu có thể. Tốc độ phản hồi là lợi thế cạnh tranh thật.
- Đừng "low-bid" mọi job. Bid đúng ngách, viết riêng từng cái. 5 proposal chất > 30 proposal copy-paste.

> 🔁 Có JD thật? Dán vào chat, tôi viết proposal đo ni đóng giày trong 1 phút.
