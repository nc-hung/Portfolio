## B. Overview (bản chính — dán nguyên)

```
I build the back-office systems that keep a business running — order and
invoice management, debt/payment reconciliation, admin dashboards, and AI
automation — and I get the hard parts right: money math, audit trails, and
a real deployment your team can use on day one.

Most "full-stack developers" can build a CRUD app. The difference shows up
when money is involved: FIFO payment allocation, multi-account reconciliation,
supplier statements, exact decimal arithmetic (never floats), and edit/undo
flows that never corrupt a balance. That's the work I specialize in.

What I can do for you:
• Order / invoice / billing systems with PDF & print-ready statements
• Customer & supplier debt tracking with automatic reconciliation
• Custom admin dashboards & internal tools (React + MUI, clean and fast)
• Excel import/export & report automation (kill the manual spreadsheet work)
• AI automation — chatbots, POS/ERP integrations, AI-generated reports
• Deployment & ops — Docker, CI/CD, backups, so it actually ships

Recent work:
• A wholesale-distribution order & debt platform — FIFO reconciliation,
  supplier statements, Excel import, print/QR invoices (Node, Express,
  Prisma/PostgreSQL, React, MUI).
• A messaging-based sales bot integrating a POS, AI sales charts, QR
  payments and automated reminders, deployed on Railway.
• An offline-first voice expense-ledger PWA (IndexedDB, service workers).

Stack: TypeScript, Node.js, Express/NestJS, React, MUI, Prisma, PostgreSQL,
SQLite, Docker, CI/CD. Comfortable integrating AI APIs (Claude, Gemini, GPT).

I work like a partner, not a ticket-taker: I ask about the business problem
first, flag edge cases before they bite, and verify with real runs before I
say "done." Clear communication, honest timelines, no disappearing.

If you have a billing/invoicing/dashboard or automation problem that has to
be correct — let's talk. Send the details and I'll tell you exactly how I'd
approach it.
```

> ✂️ Khi dùng cho **Specialized A**, cắt phần AI; khi dùng **Specialized B**, đẩy phần AI/automation lên đầu.
